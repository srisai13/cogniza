Become a Cogniza Campus Ambassador

Represent Cogniza at your college, build your leadership skills, and earn exciting rewards while helping your peers discover top-tier tech training.

Lead & Inspire

Be the voice of Cogniza on your campus and lead tech communities.

Earn Rewards

Get performance-based stipends, free courses, and exclusive swags.

Get Certified

Receive an official Letter of Recommendation and experience certificate.

Who Can Apply?

  • College students across any degree or branch.
  • Active members of tech clubs or student communities.
  • Individuals with great communication and leadership skills.
  • Students passionate about organizing events and workshops.

Your Responsibilities

  • Promote Cogniza's programs on your campus.
  • Host seminars, tech talks, and coding competitions.
  • Connect students with our career counseling team.
  • Provide on-ground feedback to improve our services.

Perks & Benefits

Enjoy exclusive benefits as you grow our community.

Exclusive Swag

Get premium Cogniza t-shirts, hoodies, and welcome kits delivered to your door.

Performance Stipend

Earn generous financial incentives based on your impact and referral success.

VIP Networking

Connect directly with tech industry leaders and get priority placement assistance.

How It Works

Your journey to becoming a Cogniza Campus Ambassador.

1

Apply Online

3

Onboarding & Swag
