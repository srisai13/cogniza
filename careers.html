Our Hiring Process

A transparent and fast four-step journey from application to offer.

1. Application

Submit your resume and portfolio. We review every application carefully.

2. Recruiter Screen

A quick 30-minute chat to discuss your background and align on expectations.

3. Technical / Task

A role-specific assessment or take-home assignment to showcase your skills.

4. Final Interview

Meet the founders and team leads. If there's a mutual fit, we make an offer!
