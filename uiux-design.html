What You Will Learn

This meticulously crafted syllabus takes you step-by-step through the core concepts required to master the domain.

01

Design Principles & Theory

Understand color theory, typography, spacing, and the psychological impact of design choices on users.

02

UI/UX Wireframing & Prototyping

Map out user journeys and build low-fidelity and high-fidelity interactive prototypes.

03

Advanced Industry Design Tools

Master Figma, Adobe XD, and Illustrator to create pixel-perfect digital assets and interfaces.

04

Real-world Industry Projects

Apply everything you have learned by building full-scale projects from scratch, simulating real client requirements.

05

Capstone Project & Certification

Complete a massive final project to showcase on your portfolio. Defend your work and earn your verified certification.

An eye for aesthetics and a creative mindset. Empathy for the end-user is critical. No prior coding experience required.
